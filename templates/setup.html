Quick Setup
  1. Copy .env.example to .env in the project folder: 
    copy .env.example .env
  2. Open .env and fill in the four required values:
Required Environment Variables
VariableDescription
SERPAPI_KEY Your SerpApi key — sign up at serpapi.com (Starter plan)
GMAIL_ADDRESS Gmail address used to send alerts
GMAIL_APP_PASSWORD 16-character Gmail App Password (requires 2FA)
ALERT_RECIPIENT Email address to receive deal alerts
Reload After Setup
